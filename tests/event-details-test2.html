About The Event

Freshman Orientation is a pivotal event designed to welcome new students to campus life. This day-long program provides valuable insights into academic programs, student resources, and extracurricular activities. You'll meet professors, make new friends, and gain confidence to start your college journey. The event also features guided campus tours, motivational talks from alumni, and engaging icebreaker sessions to foster a sense of belonging.

Join us for Freshman Orientation to get an overview of your college journey. Interact with faculty, participate in icebreaker activities, and explore the campus facilities.
